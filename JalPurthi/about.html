About
We, at Jalpurti Pipes are committed to exceeding customer expectations with producing high quality products at the cheapest price.
We Shall proactively
Adopt Zero Defect Culture through continual improvement. Strive for Zero Accidents and Incidents through engaging and consulting work-force and stakeholders. Strive for achieving Zero Energy Loss by providing necessary resource to achieve the Energy Targets & Usage of Energy efficient products and equipment. Product Environment, Prevent Pollution, Provide Safe and Healthy Working conditions by eliminating Hazards and OHS Risks. Sustain the Environment, Safety & Energy requirements through fulfilment of Compliance Obligations.
Vision We aim to be a truly global, high-performing Organisation delivering quality products and services to its customers and attain leadership position in the industries we operate in.
Mission Jalpurti Pipes is on a mission to provide clean water for everyone and everywhere; from the smallest villages to the largest cities. We are committed towards constant innovations in plumbing, irrigation and sewerage technologies to meet the world’s constantly increasing water demands.
Values
  • Customer Commitment: We believe in building long-term relationships & delighting our customers with exceptional service.
  • Quality: Deliver high quality products and services to our customers, exceeding their expectations.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standard of integrity in our actions & services. Operate in letter and spirit of the highest standards of corporate ethics.
  • Equitability & Respect: Accept criticism and promote an open culture that enables sharing of ideas across the organization. We respect & appreciate all individuals and cultural identities. We embrace the differences. We ensure a harmonious working environment for all our employees.
  • Lean Manufacturing: Minimal wastage and maximum recycling.
